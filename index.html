Welcome to my website!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Educational Psychology at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Professors Skyler T. Hawk.

My Ph.D. research focuses on the dynamic processes between adolescents and their mothers in Chinese famlies. To accomplish this, I utilize intensive longitudinal data that spans varying time intervals, ranging from days to months.


Research Interests

Dynamic of parenting adolecents, using various multilevel models such as Dynamic Strucure Equation Models.

Cultural-specific characteritics of overparenting in Chinese famlies.

Other topics about adolescent well-being.

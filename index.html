Welcome to my website!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Educational Psychology at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Professors Skyler T. Hawk.

I received a Master's degrees in Developmental Psychology from Sun Yat-sen University, examining the topics of adolescent well-being and mindifulness. I am particularly interested in multi-method approaches in research on dynamic in family process, such as associtions between overparenting and parent-child relationship quality.


Research Interests in Psychometrics

Evaluating and improving the generalizability of Structural Equation Models.

Identifying model violations in factor analysis, primarily using the Bayesian Lasso.

Other statistical techniques for psychological and educational research, such as network analysis and item response models.
